BndDelayedResponsePatternUT Globally #

Globally, it is always the case that if "R" holds, then "Q" holds after at most "5" time units for at least "10" time units

Counterexample: true;⌈R⌉;⌈true⌉ ∧ ℓ ≤ 5;⌈Q⌉ ∧ ℓ < 10;⌈!Q⌉;true
